About this resource

This dataset is intended to grow over time. It was compiled by Hannah Waller during a three-month Winter Fellowship at the Centre for the Governance of AI in early 2026, and reflects the organizations and projects she was able to identify and verify during that period. Views expressed are her own and do not represent GovAI. Corrections and additions are welcome. All submissions are reviewed before appearing in the dataset.

Please review the methodology page to understand the scope and inclusion criteria before submitting additions. Organizations outside that scope won't be added, but feedback is still welcome and may inform future iterations of this resource. If you're suggesting a new organization, the most helpful information to include is the organization's name, website, EIN, and any notable projects to spotlight, particularly if they use AI.
